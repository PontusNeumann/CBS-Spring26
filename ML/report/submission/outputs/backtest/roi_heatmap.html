Headline takeaways

  • hist_gbm is the only meaningful winner — beats naive on 4 strategies, loses on 6. Best result: top1pct_phat = +34.4% on 197 executed trades.
  • The naive baseline is small but consistently positive (+0.0% to +4.1%) — more robust than most models on broad strategies.
  • Linear models flatten to 0% on high-confidence cells because they never produce p_hat > 0.9; well-calibrated but don't make confident bets.
  • general_ev_late shows uniform +4.4% across all models — the late-deadline edge filter dominates the model choice.
  • All ML models lose 35-70% on broad strategies (top5pct_edge, general_ev, general_ev_cheap, home_run) — model edge is washed out by per-trade costs and concentration caps when the strategy bets too widely.
